My teaching experience covers a variety of subjects, including Advanced Algorithms, Machine Learning, Distributed Computer Systems, Algorithms and Data Structures, and Geographic Information Systems (GIS). Additionally, I've being a mentor to numerous Master's and PhD students. Over this period, I've published more than 50 scientific papers and served as a reviewer for multiple international journals. I had active involvement in various national and international projects and activities.
